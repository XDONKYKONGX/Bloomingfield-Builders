Modern Rear Extension with Bi-Fold Doors

This stunning contemporary rear extension in Newton Abbot redefines open-plan living. Our team at Bloomfield Builders designed and built a light-filled space featuring premium bi-fold doors that seamlessly connect the indoor living area to the garden. The project included: Structural work to create a spacious, open layout High-performance glazing for year-round comfort Sleek roofline design with minimalist detailing Premium materials matching the home's original character Energy-efficient solutions to reduce environmental impact The result is a bright, functional space that doubles the home's living area while blending modern aesthetics with the property's original charm
