Coastal-Inspired Café Transformation & Structural Remodel

Bloomfield Builders reimagined this Devon café as a tranquil coastal retreat, blending nautical elegance with structural innovation. Our team: Opened up the space with airy sightlines, mimicking the flow of the nearby shore Incorporated reclaimed driftwood and weathered oak finishes for organic texture Installed soft, ocean-toned lighting (think seaglass blues and sandy neutrals) Designed custom booth seating with wave-inspired curves and linen upholstery Added subtle maritime details: rope accents, porthole mirrors, and shell motifs The refurbishment honours Devon's seaside heritage while improving functionality—expanding seating by 10% and creating a calming, sunlit environment that guests adore.

Serving all of Devon including:  Exeter, Plymouth, Torquay, Newton Abbot  and throughout the Teign Valley.
